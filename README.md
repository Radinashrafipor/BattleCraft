# ⚔️ BATTLECRAFT: Modern Warfare Evolved

---

### **Overview**
**BattleCraft** is a definitive tactical overhaul for Minecraft. Designed for players who crave authentic, high-stakes military action, this modpack transforms your blocky world into a theater of modern conflict. 

Forget wooden swords and bows; it’s time to gear up with state-of-the-art armaments, realistic ballistics, and high-intensity combat mechanics.

---

### **🛡️ Core Features**
*   **Realistic Gunplay:** Powered by the *Superb Warfare* mod, experience a fully realized firearm system. Engage in tactical firefights with realistic ballistics, recoil management, and weapon customization.
*   **Tactical Expansion:** We have curated a suite of *SPW Addons* to ensure your arsenal is always ready for the next deployment.
*   **Immersive Warfare:** From urban clearance operations to long-range reconnaissance, every aspect of your survival gameplay is shifted into a modern theater of war.

---

### **🚀 Optimization Suite**
We understand that high-quality assets require high-quality performance. *BattleCraft* comes pre-configured with essential optimization mods:

1.  `Embeddium`: High-performance engine rendering for stable FPS during intense combat.
2.  `FerriteCore`: Optimized memory usage to ensure your game stays responsive.
3.  `ModernFix`: Backend improvements to keep the pack stable across diverse hardware configurations.

### **📋 Requirements & Recommendations**
*   **RAM Allocation:** We recommend assigning at least **4GB to 6GB of RAM** for the best experience.
*   **Java Version:** Ensure you are running the recommended Java version for your specific Minecraft release.
*   **Shaders:** While optional, *BattleCraft* is compatible with most performance-friendly shader packs.
